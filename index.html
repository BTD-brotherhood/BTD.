The House of memories

BTD isn't just a group—it's a family full of fun, laughter, and crazy memories. Each member has a special role that makes the gang complete.

💻 Adiraj – The Designer & Developer
The tech genius. Whether it's making cool posters or fixing a game, he's the mastermind behind all things creative.

🤝 Angshuman – The Helper
The most reliable one. Need notes? A ride? A last-minute favor? He's always there to help, no questions asked.

😜 Ishandeep – The Naughty Boy
The troublemaker. His pranks, jokes, and funny tricks make every day exciting. From sneaky plans to hilarious moments, he keeps the gang entertained.

📚 Dawar – The Topper
The brainiac. While others mess around, he's the one scoring top marks. But he also helps his friends with studies—if they stop distracting him!

😎 Prabal – The Flirty Boy
Smooth with the ladies. He can turn any moment into a flirty conversation, and his confidence is unmatched.

🌟 Gaurav – The Center of Attraction
Loud, funny, and full of energy—wherever he goes, all eyes follow.

🔥 Together, they make BTD—more than a gang, a lifelong bond!
